The plant is Tagetes erecta

[ Attach Photo of Marigold ]
Classification.
Kingdom: Plantae
Division: Angiosperms
Class: Eudicots
Order: Asterales
Family: Asteraceae
Genus: Tagetes
Species: erecta
  • It is an ornamental flowering plant known for its bright yellow and orange blooms.
  • The plant is widely used in India for making garlands and festival decorations.
  • Marigolds have a pungent scent that acts as a natural repellent against insects.
  • It is a hardy plant that thrives in full sunlight and well-drained soil.
  • The flowers are used to extract natural food coloring and dyes.

The plant is Solanum melongena

[ Attach Photo of Brinjal/Eggplant ]
Classification.
Kingdom: Plantae
Division: Angiosperms
Class: Eudicots
Order: Solanales
Family: Solanaceae
Genus: Solanum
Species: melongena
  • Commonly known as Brinjal or Eggplant, it is a popular vegetable crop.
  • The plant is a delicate perennial that is often grown as an annual in temperate climates.
  • The fruit is botanically classified as a berry and contains numerous small seeds.
  • The skin of the fruit is typically glossy and ranges from deep purple to green or white.
  • It belongs to the "nightshade" family, which also includes tomatoes and potatoes.

The plant is Rosa indica

[ Attach Photo of Rose ]
Classification.
Kingdom: Plantae
Division: Angiosperms
Class: Eudicots
Order: Rosales
Family: Rosaceae
Genus: Rosa
Species: indica
  • The Rose is known as the "Queen of Flowers" due to its beauty and fragrance.
  • The stems are usually armed with sharp prickles (commonly called thorns).
  • Leaves are alternate and pinnately compound with serrated edges.
  • Roses are widely cultivated for their ornamental value and for making perfumes.
  • Rose water and "Gulkand" are popular products derived from its petals.
